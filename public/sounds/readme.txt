
Sound effects for the Axium platform UI:

- hover.mp3: Soft click sound for hovering over interactive elements
- click.mp3: Button click sound
- switch-on.mp3: Toggle switch turning on (for dark mode)
- switch-off.mp3: Toggle switch turning off (for light mode)
- success.mp3: Success notification sound
- error.mp3: Error notification sound
- notification.mp3: General notification sound

These sounds need to be added to the public/sounds directory. The files should be small in size
and have a subtle, professional sound quality appropriate for a financial/trading platform.
